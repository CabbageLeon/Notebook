## 带电系统的电场能量
### 能量密度
$$
\begin{align}
W_e &= \frac{1}{2} \int_V (\nabla \cdot \vec D) \phi dV \\ &=\frac{1}{2} \int_V \rho \phi dV \\
&= \frac{1}{2} \int_V [\nabla \cdot (\phi \vec D)-\nabla \phi \cdot \vec D]dV \\ &= \frac{1}{2} \oint \phi \vec D \cdot \vec{ds}+\frac{1}{2} \int_V \vec{E} \cdot \vec D dv
\end{align}
$$
